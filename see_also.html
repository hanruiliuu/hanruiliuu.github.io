How do people celebrate Lunar New Year?

Lunar New Year is celebrated with various customs and traditions that vary by country and region. Some common practices include:

- Family Reunions: Families gather together to celebrate the festival, often traveling long distances to be with loved ones.
- Red Packets: Adults give children red envelopes filled with money as a symbol of good luck and prosperity.
- Festive Foods: Special dishes are prepared for the occasion, such as dumplings, fish, and sticky rice cakes.
